About Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is an iconic Indian dish with a creamy, tomato-based sauce. It combines grilled chicken tikka with a smooth, aromatic gravy. It's rich, comforting, and absolutely delicious.

Ingredients (Chicken Tikka)

  • 600g Chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tablespoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients (Masala Sauce)

  • 4 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 large Onion (sliced)
  • 3 tablespoons Butter
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tablespoon Garam Masala
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh Coriander (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chilli powder, and lemon juice.
  2. Marinate chicken for 2 hours.
  3. Thread chicken onto skewers and grill or bake at 200¬∞C for 20 minutes.
  4. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates (5-7 minutes).
  7. Add garam masala, cumin, and chilli powder. Mix well.
  8. Add grilled chicken tikka to the sauce.
  9. Stir in heavy cream and simmer for 10 minutes.
  10. Adjust salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Basmati Rice
  • Pair with Garlic Naan
  • Accompany with side salad
