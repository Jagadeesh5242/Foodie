Butter Chicken

⏱ 45 Minutes
🍗 Non-Veg
🔥 Medium
Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is one of the most famous North Indian dishes. Tender chicken pieces are cooked in a rich tomato-butter gravy with cream and aromatic spices, creating a smooth and flavorful curry loved worldwide.

🧂 Ingredients (Serves 4)

🍗 500g Chicken (boneless)
🥛 1 cup Yogurt
🍅 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
🧄 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
🧈 2 tbsp Butter
🥛 3 tbsp Fresh Cream
🌶️ 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
🌿 1 tsp Garam Masala
🧂 Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Watch Video Guide

👨‍🍳 Cooking Process

1 Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, chilli powder and salt for 30 minutes.
2 Cook the marinated chicken in a pan until tender.
3 In another pan heat butter and add tomato puree.
4 Cook until oil separates and add spices.
5 Add cooked chicken pieces into the gravy.
6 Add fresh cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
7 Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
