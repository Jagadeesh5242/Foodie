Egg Fried Rice

⏱ 20 Minutes
🥚 Egg Dish
🔥 Easy
Egg Fried Rice is a quick and flavorful Indo-Chinese dish made with stir-fried rice, scrambled eggs, vegetables, and savory sauces. It is perfect for busy weeknights and pairs well with chili chicken, manchurian, or spicy gravy dishes.

🧂 Ingredients (Serves 3)

🍚 3 cups Cooked Rice (preferably cold)
🥚 3 Eggs
🥕 1 Carrot (finely chopped)
🫘 ½ cup Beans
🧅 1 Onion (chopped)
🧄 1 tbsp Garlic (minced)
🥬 Spring Onions
🥢 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
🌶️ 1 tsp Black Pepper
🧂 Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Watch Video Guide

👨‍🍳 Cooking Process

1 Heat oil in a wok and scramble the eggs. Set aside.
2 In the same wok sauté garlic and onions for a few seconds.
3 Add carrots, beans and stir fry on high flame.
4 Add cooked rice and mix well with vegetables.
5 Add soy sauce, salt and black pepper.
6 Add scrambled eggs and mix thoroughly.
7 Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
