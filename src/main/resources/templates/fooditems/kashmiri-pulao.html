Kashmiri Pulao

Kashmiri Pulao is a fragrant and mildly sweet rice dish from the beautiful valley of Kashmir. Unlike spicy biryanis, this pulao is known for its delicate aroma, rich saffron flavor, and the use of dry fruits and fresh fruits. The dish combines basmati rice with nuts like cashews and almonds, raisins, and fruits such as apples and pomegranate seeds. Cooked in aromatic spices and garnished with saffron milk, Kashmiri Pulao offers a royal flavor and vibrant colors that make it perfect for festive meals and special occasions.

🧂 Ingredients (Serves 4)

🍚 2 cups Basmati Rice
🥛 2 tbsp Milk with Saffron
🥜 Cashews & Almonds
🍇 Raisins
🍎 Apple Cubes
🍇 Pomegranate Seeds
🧈 2 tbsp Ghee
🌿 Whole Spices (Clove, Cardamom, Cinnamon)
🧅 1 Onion (sliced)
🧂 Salt & Sugar (optional)

👨‍🍳 Cooking Process

1 Wash and soak basmati rice for about 30 minutes. Cook the rice until fluffy and keep it aside.
2 Heat ghee in a pan and fry cashews, almonds, and raisins until golden. Remove and keep them aside.
3 In the same pan add whole spices such as cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon.
4 Add sliced onions and sauté until lightly golden.
5 Add cooked rice, saffron milk, salt, and a small pinch of sugar. Mix gently.
6 Add fried dry fruits and apple cubes. Stir gently so the rice grains remain separate.
7 Garnish with pomegranate seeds and serve hot with raita.
