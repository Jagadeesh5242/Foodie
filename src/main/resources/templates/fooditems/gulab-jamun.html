Gulab Jamun

⏱ 30 Minutes
🍬 Dessert
🔥 Easy
Gulab Jamun is one of India’s most beloved desserts. These soft, deep-fried milk dumplings are soaked in fragrant sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water. Served warm, they melt in the mouth and are a must-have during festivals and celebrations.

🧂 Ingredients (Serves 4)

🥛 1 cup Milk Powder
🌾 2 tbsp All Purpose Flour (Maida)
🥛 2 tbsp Milk
🧈 1 tsp Ghee
🧂 A pinch Baking Soda
🍬 1½ cups Sugar
💧 1½ cups Water
🌿 4 Cardamom Pods

👩‍🍳 Watch Video Guide

👨‍🍳 Cooking Process

1 Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, ghee and milk to form a soft dough.
2 Divide the dough and roll into small smooth balls.
3 Heat oil or ghee on low flame and fry the balls until golden brown.
4 Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water and cardamom.
5 Add fried gulab jamun into warm syrup.
6 Let them soak for 1–2 hours for best flavor.
7 Serve warm and garnish with pistachios.
