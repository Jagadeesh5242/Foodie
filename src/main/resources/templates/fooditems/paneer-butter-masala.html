Kaju Paneer Butter Masala

⏱ 35 Minutes
🥦 Vegetarian
🔥 Medium Difficulty
Kaju Paneer Butter Masala is a luxurious North Indian curry made with soft paneer cubes cooked in a creamy tomato and cashew gravy. The richness of butter, cream, and cashew paste gives the dish its signature silky texture and mildly sweet flavor. It is one of the most popular restaurant-style paneer dishes and pairs perfectly with naan, roti, or jeera rice.

🧂 Ingredients (Serves 3-4)

🧀 250g Paneer (cubed)
🥜 15–20 Cashews (soaked & blended to paste)
🍅 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
🧅 1 Onion (finely chopped)
🧄 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
🧈 2 tbsp Butter
🥛 3 tbsp Fresh Cream
🌶️ 1 tsp Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder
🌿 1 tsp Garam Masala
🧂 Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Watch Video Guide

👨‍🍳 Cooking Process

1 Soak cashews in warm water for 15 minutes and grind them into a smooth paste.
2 Heat butter in a pan and sauté chopped onions until soft and translucent.
3 Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute until aromatic.
4 Add tomato puree, chilli powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
5 Add cashew paste and mix well to create a rich creamy gravy.
6 Add paneer cubes and simmer for 4-5 minutes.
7 Add cream and garam masala, cook for another 2 minutes and serve hot.
