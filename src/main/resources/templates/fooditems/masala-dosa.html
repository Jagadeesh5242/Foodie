Ragi Masala Dosa

⏱ 40 Minutes
🥦 Vegetarian
🔥 Medium Difficulty
Ragi Masala Dosa is a healthy twist to the classic South Indian dosa. Made with nutrient-rich ragi (finger millet) flour, this crispy dosa is packed with fiber, calcium, and iron. It is usually served with spicy potato masala filling, coconut chutney, and sambar.

🧂 Ingredients (Serves 3-4)

🌾 1 cup Ragi Flour
🍚 1 cup Rice Flour
🥛 ½ cup Curd (optional)
🧂 Salt to taste
🧅 1 Onion (finely chopped)
🌶️ 2 Green Chillies
🥔 3 Boiled Potatoes
🌿 Curry Leaves & Coriander

👩‍🍳 Watch Video Guide

👨‍🍳 Cooking Process

1 Mix ragi flour, rice flour, curd, salt and water to make a smooth dosa batter.
2 Heat a dosa pan and spread the batter thinly to form a crispy dosa.
3 Prepare potato masala by sautéing onions, green chillies and curry leaves.
4 Add mashed boiled potatoes and mix with spices.
5 Place the potato masala in the center of the dosa.
6 Fold the dosa and cook until crisp.
7 Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
