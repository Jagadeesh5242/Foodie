    Garlic Naan

    Soft, aromatic flatbread with garlic and butter

    About

    Garlic Naan is a beloved Indian flatbread brushed with garlic-infused butter. Its soft and pillowy texture, combined with the savory aroma of garlic, makes it perfect for any curry or as a standalone snack.

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
    • 1/2 cup Yogurt
    • 1/4 tablespoon Sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon Baking Powder
    • 3 tablespoons Oil
    • Water as needed
    • 3 tablespoons Butter, melted
    • 6 cloves Garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons Fresh Coriander, chopped

    Instructions

    1. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar in a bowl.
    2. Add yogurt and oil to the dry ingredients.
    3. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough.
    4. Cover and let rest for 2-3 hours.
    5. Divide dough into 6-8 portions and shape into balls.
    6. Roll each ball into a thin oval or round shape.
    7. Heat a tawa or cast iron skillet over high heat.
    8. Place naan on the hot tawa and cook until light brown spots appear.
    9. Flip and cook the other side until puffed and cooked.
    10. Mix melted butter with minced garlic and chopped coriander.
    11. Brush the garlic-butter mixture on hot naan.
    12. Serve immediately while warm.
