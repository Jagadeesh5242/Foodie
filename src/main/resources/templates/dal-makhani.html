Creamy lentils with butter and cream

About Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani is a luxurious lentil curry made with black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked and finished with butter and cream. It's rich, creamy, and a restaurant favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Black Lentils (Urad Dal)
  • 1/4 cup Red Kidney Beans (Rajma)
  • 4 tablespoons Butter
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • 1.5 tablespoons Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chillies (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Soak lentils and kidney beans separately for 4 hours.
  2. Pressure cook until very soft (about 45 minutes).
  3. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chillies. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates (5-7 minutes).
  6. Add coriander, cumin, chilli, and turmeric powders. Mix well.
  7. Add the cooked dal and kidney beans to the pan.
  8. Add salt and mix well.
  9. Simmer for 20 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally.
  10. Add heavy cream and stir well.
  11. Simmer for another 10 minutes until creamy and aromatic.
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Basmati Rice
  • Pair with Garlic Naan
  • Garnish with dollop of cream
