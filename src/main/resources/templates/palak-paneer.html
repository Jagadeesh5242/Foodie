Creamy spinach curry with cottage cheese

About Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a vegetarian delight made with spinach puree and cottage cheese. It's creamy, healthy, and packed with nutrients. A favorite in Indian restaurants and home kitchens alike.

Ingredients

  • 500g Fresh Spinach (chopped)
  • 250g Paneer (cottage cheese, cubed)
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 tablespoons Ghee or Butter
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chillies (chopped)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Boil spinach in water until soft (3-4 minutes).
  2. Drain and cool, then blend into a smooth puree.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  4. Saut√© onions until translucent.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chillies. Cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add spinach puree and stir well.
  7. Cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.
  8. Add garam masala, salt, and black pepper.
  9. Stir in heavy cream and mix well.
  10. Gently add paneer cubes.
  11. Simmer for 5-7 minutes without stirring much.
  12. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Garlic Naan
  • Pair with Basmati Rice
  • Garnish with fresh cream
