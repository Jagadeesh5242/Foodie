    Palak Paneer

    Creamy spinach curry with soft cottage cheese cubes

    About

    Palak Paneer is a beloved vegetarian dish combining fresh spinach with paneer cheese. The creamy spinach puree blended with aromatic spices and finished with cream creates a nutritious, indulgent curry.

    Ingredients

    • 500g Fresh Spinach, chopped
    • 250g Paneer, cubed
    • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
    • 2 tablespoons Ghee
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
    • 2 Green Chilies, chopped
    • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala
    • Salt and Pepper to taste

    Instructions

    1. Boil spinach in water until soft, about 4 minutes.
    2. Drain and blend into a smooth puree.
    3. Heat ghee and add cumin seeds.
    4. Saut√© onions until translucent.
    5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for 1 minute.
    6. Pour in spinach puree and stir well.
    7. Cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.
    8. Add garam masala, salt, and pepper.
    9. Stir in heavy cream.
    10. Gently add paneer cubes and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
    11. Serve hot with naan or rice.
