Crispy fried pastry with spiced potato filling

About Samosa

Samosa is one of India's most beloved snacks. It's a triangular pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas, deep-fried until golden and crispy. Perfect for tea time or as an appetizer.

Ingredients (Dough)

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 4 tablespoons Oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon Carom Seeds (Ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Warm water as needed

Ingredients (Filling)

  • 4 large Potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 cup Peas (cooked)
  • 2 Green Chillies (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons Oil

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, carom seeds, salt, and oil until breadcrumb consistency.
  2. Add warm water and knead into a firm dough.
  3. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  4. For filling: Heat oil and saut√© ginger and green chillies.
  5. Add mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Mix well. Cool.
  6. Divide dough into 12 balls. Roll each into a thin circle.
  7. Cut each circle in half and shape into a cone.
  8. Fill with potato mixture and seal edges with water.
  9. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
  10. Fry samosas until golden brown on both sides.
  11. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Green Chutney
  • Pair with Tamarind Chutney
  • Enjoy with Hot Chai (Tea)
