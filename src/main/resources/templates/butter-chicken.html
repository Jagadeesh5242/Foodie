Creamy, rich, and delicious tomato-based curry

About Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) is one of India's most popular dishes. Tender chicken in a smooth, creamy tomato sauce with butter and cream. Perfect for special occasions and easy weeknight dinners.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken (boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tablespoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 3 tablespoons Butter
  • 1 large Onion (sliced)
  • 4 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 tablespoons Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and chilli powder for 2 hours.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and fry marinated chicken until cooked. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, saut√© onions until golden.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes until oil separates.
  5. Add cream and mix well.
  6. Add cooked chicken back to the pan.
  7. Add kasuri methi and simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Garlic Naan
  • Pair with Basmati Rice
  • Add side of sliced onions
