Kidney bean curry with tomato gravy

About Rajma

Rajma is a popular North Indian curry made with kidney beans cooked in a tomato-based gravy. It's healthy, protein-rich, and usually served with rice. A comfort food favorite in many Indian homes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Kidney Beans (soaked overnight and cooked)
  • 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tablespoons Oil
  • 1 large Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chillies (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Soak kidney beans overnight and pressure cook until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  3. Saut√© onions until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chillies. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates (5-7 minutes).
  6. Add coriander, chilli, turmeric, and garam masala powders.
  7. Add cooked kidney beans to the curry.
  8. Add salt and mix well.
  9. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  10. The curry should be thick and aromatic.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander.
  12. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Steamed White Rice
  • Pair with Roti or Chapati
  • Accompany with Pickle
  • Serve with Sliced Onions
