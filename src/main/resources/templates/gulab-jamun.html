Sweet Fried Milk Solid Dumplings in Syrup

About This Dish

Gulab Jamun is an iconic Indian sweet made from milk solids and flour, shaped into small balls, deep fried, and then soaked in rose-flavored sugar syrup. It's a favorite at celebrations and weddings across India.
