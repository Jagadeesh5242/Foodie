Dry curry of potatoes and cauliflower

About Aloo Gobi

Aloo Gobi is a popular Indian vegetable curry made with potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi). It's dry, aromatic, and packed with spices. Perfect as a side dish or main course.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium Potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 medium Cauliflower (cut into florets)
  • 3 tablespoons Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  • 1 large Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Green Chillies (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan/wok.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger and green chillies, cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add cubed potatoes and stir well.
  6. Add turmeric, coriander, and chilli powder. Mix well.
  7. Cook on high heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Reduce heat to medium and add cauliflower florets.
  9. Add salt and garam masala.
  10. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  11. The vegetables should be tender and slightly caramelized.
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Chapati or Roti
  • Pair with Rice and Dal
  • Accompany with Pickle and Yogurt
