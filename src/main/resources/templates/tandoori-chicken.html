About Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori Chicken is a classic Indian appetizer. Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a tandoor (clay oven) until charred and smoky. It's healthy, flavorful, and perfect for dinner parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 tablespoons Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tablespoons Lemon Juice
  • 2 tablespoons Garam Masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons Oil or Ghee
  • Fresh Coriander

Instructions

  1. Make slits in chicken pieces for better marination.
  2. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder, turmeric, and lemon juice.
  3. Add garam masala, cumin seeds, and salt.
  4. Coat chicken thoroughly with the marinade.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Preheat oven to 200¬∞C or heat tandoor.
  7. Thread chicken onto skewers.
  8. Cook in oven or tandoor for 30-40 minutes until cooked through.
  9. Baste with oil/ghee halfway through cooking.
  10. Chicken should be golden and charred on edges.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Mint Chutney
  • Pair with Lemon Wedges
  • Accompany with Sliced Onions
  • Serve with Naan Bread
