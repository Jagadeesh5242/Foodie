Grilled cottage cheese with aromatic spices

About Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a vegetarian appetizer made with cottage cheese marinated in yogurt and spices, then grilled or baked. It's healthy, protein-rich, and perfect for parties or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 400g Paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tablespoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tablespoons Lemon Juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 Bell Peppers (chunked)
  • 1 large Onion (chunked)
  • Fresh Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chilli powder, turmeric, and lemon juice.
  2. Add salt and mix well to make the marinade.
  3. Add paneer cubes, bell peppers, and onions to the marinade.
  4. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  5. Thread the marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers alternately.
  6. Grill in an oven at 200¬∞C for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway.
  7. Alternatively, pan-fry in a greased pan until golden.
  8. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with mint chutney.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with mint chutney
  • Pair with lemon wedges
  • Serve as appetizer before main course
