Fluffy fried bread with spiced chickpeas

About Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian breakfast dish. Bhature is a soft, fluffy fried bread served with Chole (spiced chickpeas). It's hearty, filling, and a favorite in Punjab.

Ingredients (Bhature Dough)

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • Warm water as needed

Ingredients (Chole Curry)

  • 2 cans Chickpeas (or 1 cup dried, soaked and cooked)
  • 3 tablespoons Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. Add yogurt and oil. Mix well.
  3. Add warm water gradually and knead into soft dough.
  4. Cover and let it rest for 4-6 hours or overnight.
  5. For chole: Heat oil and add cumin seeds.
  6. Saut√© onions until golden brown.
  7. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for 1 minute.
  8. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  9. Add spices and cook for 2 minutes.
  10. Add chickpeas and simmer for 15 minutes. Add salt.
  11. Divide dough into 8 balls. Roll each into a circle.
  12. Heat oil in a deep pan and deep fry until puffed and golden.
  13. Serve hot bhature with chole curry on the side.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Chole Curry
  • Pair with Mint Chutney
  • Add sliced onions and lemon
