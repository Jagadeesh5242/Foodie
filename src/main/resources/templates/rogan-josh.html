Aromatic meat curry with yogurt and tomatoes

About Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a classic Kashmiri curry made with meat (usually lamb or chicken). The name means "meat in fragrant gravy." It's aromatic, flavorful, and features a blend of traditional spices with yogurt and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 700g Lamb or Mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups Yogurt
  • 3 large Onions (sliced)
  • 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 3 tablespoons Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 4 Green Cardamom Pods
  • 2 Black Cardamom Pods
  • 3 Cloves
  • 1 inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 Star Anise
  • 2 tablespoons Coriander Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Cumin Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric
  • 3 tablespoons Oil or Ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pot. Add whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaf, star anise).
  2. Saut√© onions until golden brown. Remove half and set aside.
  3. Add meat to the remaining onions and cook until partially done (5 minutes).
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add yogurt and stir well to combine.
  6. Add tomato puree and ground spices (coriander, cumin, chilli, turmeric).
  7. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add salt and reserved onions on top.
  9. Cover the pot and simmer on low heat for 1-1.5 hours until meat is tender.
  10. Stir occasionally and check if additional water is needed.
  11. The curry should be thick and aromatic.
  12. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with Basmati Rice or Pulao
  • Pair with Garlic Naan or Roti
  • Garnish with fresh coriander
